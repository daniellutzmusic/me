Daniel Lutz

Romantic Symbolist Songwriter

Lover of wisdom

At Odds With The Empire

an ariose missionary and logger of wisdom pearls

“my music evokes a sense of old-world exploration through oceanic rhythms alongside yearning melodies

that evoke the harsh realities of a society and individual without beauty and order—

thru song I am grasping for eternal truths that can be held in

the palm of our soul

I seek musical wisdom — I aim to reconcile opposing forces — (I believe that music contains answers)

the realm of towering dreams and primordial nightmares — the exquisite and the homely — what enlightens and what paralyzes

the ideal listener will be inspired to rise and vanquish his eartly demons!

the music implores you to connect and to persevere!

I write music meant to inspire timeless pursuits

I seek God

I aim to make music that pleases god

My music is an expression of longing for a golden age where goodness, beauty and truth are valued above all

My music ponders a potential world in tune — not through social engineering and tyranny, but through reconciliation

and . .
